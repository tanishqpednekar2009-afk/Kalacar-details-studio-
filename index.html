Our Story

BUILT FOR
PERFECTION

Kalacar Detailing Studio was founded with one obsession — to treat every vehicle as a masterpiece. Located in the heart of Mulund West, we have become Mumbai's most trusted name for paint protection, ceramic coatings, and precision detailing.

From daily drivers to exotic machines, every car and bike that enters our studio receives relentless attention to detail. Our team holds itself to a standard that goes beyond the industry norm — because your vehicle deserves nothing less.
